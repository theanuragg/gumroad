Rate and review your purchase

Leaving a written review is the best way to give product feedback to both the creator and future potential customers.

Leaving a review

After you've purchased a product, you can leave feedback from several different locations:

  1. Your Reviews page, which allows you to review multiple purchases in one location. From here, you can also edit previous reviews.
  2. The content page of the product
  3. The product page

If you haven't left a review in the first 5 days after a purchase, we will send you a reminder email. You can unsubscribe from these reminders using the link at the bottom of the email.


Restrictions

  1. You can only leave a review in the first year after the purchase was made
  2. For membership purchases with free trials, you cannot leave a review until the free trial has expired
  3. You cannot leave a review on refunded purchases (and reviews will be removed after a full refund)
