Gift cards and prepaid cards

We do accept prepaid and gift cards, however, because these cards are often used for fraud, the card companies that back them often do not recognize or have not yet authorized Gumroad to process their cards, and thus may prevent us from charging the card.

Is Gumroad safe?

Yes.

Our payment is secured by SSL with 128-bit encryption and processed with PCI Compliant service providers. Find out more here.

Do I need to have a Gumroad account to buy products?

Nope! We want things to be as simple as possible for you, so just pay for the product and be done with it. We will send you an email receipt that has the link to your content in it.

If you want to create an account after the purchase to access your files, you can do that here. Be sure to sign up with the email address you used to buy the products.

Understanding the payment form

  1. Email address: This is where you will receive a receipt, invoice, and download link for the product you buy.
  2. Give as a gift: See the toggle here? You can click this to send this product to a friend. This will send the receipt to you, but the product to your friend. Read more here. Keep in mind that you currently cannot gift memberships.
  3. Discount code:* You may not see this box if it's not turned on for this product. A discount code is a discount that the creator of this product may or may not have advertised. If you know of one to enter, enter it into the discount code box. Wait for a maximum of 3 seconds to see the price change.
  4. Mailed to:* If the product you are looking at is displaying this field, you need to enter your shipping address. If your country does not have ZIP Codes (and most don't) simply enter your Postal Code.
  5. Card Number: This is what it's all been building up to: Entering those sweet, sweet credit card digits. Depending on the policies of your credit card provider, you may be asked for more information to ensure that your purchase is not fraudulent.
  6. Business VAT ID*: You can also enter a valid VAT or ABN ID if you're in the EU / UK or Australia to get the VAT refunded. You will also be able to refund the VAT through the link in your receipt. If our system detects your IP address to be outside of the EU / UK you will not see this on your checkout form and you will not be able to refund the VAT to yourself, because you will not have paid any VAT.

*You may or may not see this field, depending on what kind of product it is, where you are or how the creator set it up.

Common errors

If you do not fill in all the fields of your payment form, they will appear highlighted, and prohibit you from proceeding. However, there may be something else amiss.

For more information, see this article. Briefly, though, you may be blocked for one of the following reasons:

  1. CVC Code: If you enter the wrong CVC code, your payment will not process. Make sure all your credit card details are correct before clicking "pay."
  2. Zip code verification: You may find a pop-up window asking you for your "zip code." When this happens, your credit card provider is communicating with our processing engine, asking us to verify that you are the person using this card. If you don't live in the United States, enter your Postal Code without any spaces. For example, ”BH13 6BD” should be entered as ”BH136BD”.
  3. Provider policies: If you don't receive a specific error message, your credit card provider may be blocking your purchase from finalizing. Usually, a quick call to them will solve this. Just ask them to "whitelist" Gumroad.com, and attempt your purchase again.

Discount codes

To reiterate, at the checkout screen, you may see a box appear where you can enter a discount code. If you are aware of a discount code that this creator has advertised, enter that now and wait a maximum of 3 seconds for the price to change.

After a successful purchase

You will see a success message and your receipt will be emailed to you.

Double-check the email address here. Is there a typo? Then copy the wrong email address and let us know the misspelled email and to which email address the product should be sent. If you don't know the misspelled email address, don't worry. We have other ways of finding your purchase, and we will be happy to fix it for you.

Your purchase is complete

After you've bought a product on Gumroad, you can enter a password to sign up for a Gumroad account. Why would you do this? To take advantage of the Gumroad Library. The Library allows you to access your product at any time by logging into Gumroad. If you are buying a membership, you have to create a Gumroad account at the time of purchase.

From your Gumroad receipt, you can also easily repurchase a product, download the product, or open it in our iOS or Android app.
